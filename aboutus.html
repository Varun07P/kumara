Kumara Engineering Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was formally incepted in 2023, inheriting the operational legacy and deep expertise of Sunrise Enterprises, established in 2011. We are the true leaders in end-to-end power distribution, control systems, and turnkey electrical solutions.

Our Story

Built on a foundation of technical mastery and unwavering commitment

Managed by Mr. Kumaraswamy B.R., a professionally qualified Electrical Engineer with 16+ years of vast experience in Electrical Engineering and allied fields, our company has established itself as a reputed manufacturer and supplier of premium quality customized panels.

We specialize in distribution panels, star delta panels, MCC and PCC panels, bus ducts, LT panels, APFC panels, ATS panels, fire protection panels, lighting panels, DG synchronizing panels, and PLC control panels. Known for their high performance, less maintenance, easy operation, sturdy construction, and long service life.

Our products are manufactured using cutting-edge technology and high-quality raw material as per set industry standards including IS and IEC 61439 Part I and II. To meet diverse client needs, we offer products in several technical specifications.

Manufacturing Facility
