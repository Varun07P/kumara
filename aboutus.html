About KES

End-to-End Electrical Engineering

Kumara Engineering Solutions Private Limited was formally incepted in 2023, inheriting the operational legacy and deep expertise of Sunrise Enterprises, established in 2011. We are a reputed manufacturer and supplier of a premium quality range of customized electrical panels — MCC, PCC, LT, APFC, ATS, DG Sync, PLC, HVAC, Bus Duct, and more.

Known for high performance, less maintenance, easy operation, sturdy construction, corrosion resistance, and long service life, our products are manufactured using cutting-edge technology and high-quality raw materials as per IEC 61439 and IS:8623 standards. Operating from our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Bengaluru, Karnataka, we serve industries across the region.
