About KES

End-to-End Electrical Engineering

Kumara Engineering Solutions Private Limited was formally incorporated in 2023, inheriting the operational legacy and deep expertise of Sunrise Enterprises, established in 2011. We manufacture and supply a premium range of customised electrical panels — MCC, PCC, LT, APFC, ATS, DG Sync, PLC, HVAC, Bus Duct, and more.

Our products are built to IEC 61439 and IS:8623 standards using cutting-edge technology and quality raw materials. Known for high performance, low maintenance, and long service life, we operate from our state-of-the-art facility in Bengaluru, Karnataka.
