How We Work

Our Project Execution Process

A rigorous 7-step methodology that keeps quality, safety and schedule in check on every site.

Step 01

Feasibility & Planning

Site survey, existing infrastructure assessment, load estimation, risk analysis and a complete Project Execution Plan with BOQ within 2 working days.

Step 02

Design Management

SLDs, lighting layouts, power distribution drawings, BIM coordination and authority submission packages — all clash-detected and approval-ready.

Step 03

Statutory Approvals

CEIG, NOC, fire authority and utility authority approvals — fully managed by our licensed team. You focus on your project; we handle the paperwork.

Step 04

Procurement

Technical submittals, vendor evaluation, Factory Acceptance Tests (FAT) for panels and transformers, and tight logistics management to avoid site delays.

Step 05

Construction & Installation

On-site mobilisation, supervised installation of all electrical, HVAC, fire, plumbing and civil systems — with daily progress reports (DPR) issued to the client.

Step 06

Testing & Commissioning

Insulation tests, earth resistance verification, functional panel tests, load trials and full performance validation — with written commissioning reports for every system.

Step 07

Handover & Documentation

As-built drawings, O&M manuals, warranty documents, staff training records and a complete digital + hardcopy handover dossier. Project closed cleanly.
