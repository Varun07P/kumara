Manufacturing Stages

The 6-Step Workflow

Precision executed at every stage ensuring safety, longevity, and IEC compliance.

01

Design & Estimation

Our in-house engineering team works closely with factory requirements to draft precise Single Line Diagrams (SLDs) and fully realized layout plans using advanced CAD tools.

02

Fabrication & Coating

Premium CRCA sheet steel is rigorously fabricated, folded, and passed through a 9-tank pre-treatment anti-corrosion process before receiving a thick RAL 7032/7035 powder coating.

03

Switchgear Assembly

The panel backbone. Internal busbar routing is implemented utilizing highly conductive Copper or Aluminium rated precisely per IS standards, with safety torque marking visible.

04

Wiring & Harnessing

Immaculate control logic wiring using flame-retardant (FRLS) wires. Neat PVC channel routing with distinct color coding makes later troubleshooting and maintenance entirely straightforward.

05

Testing & Q/C

Safety is non-negotiable. Every delivered panel is subjected to rigorous high-voltage tests, Megger insulation tests, and routine functional checks before earning its internal QC passing tag.

06

Dispatch & Handover

Once certified, the panels are safely packed for transit. Our certified erection engineers manage onsite placement, perform commissioning, and navigate the final CEIG handover.
