How We Build

Our Manufacturing Process

A disciplined 6-step process — from your requirement to a fully tested, shipped panel with traceability at every stage.

01

Planning & Evaluation

We review your requirement, run a complete cost analysis and issue an Order Acknowledgement with full panel specification within 2 working days.

02

Panel Design

Auto-generated layout drawings, component placement, power and control schematics — with a bookmarked engineering submittal PDF for client approval before production begins.

03

Material Procurement

After QC sign-off on the design, we raise POs to approved OEM vendors with confirmed pricing and a delivery schedule tied to the production start date.

04

Fabrication & Assembly

CNC/hydraulic sheet metal cutting, DIN rail installation, wire processing, multi-colour labelling and full component assembly — per the approved design drawings.

05

QA, Testing & Dispatch

Full point-to-point wire testing, HV dielectric tests, functional checks, torque verification — then careful packaging with Drop N' Tell and Tip N' Tell indicators before dispatch.

06

On-Site Installation & Support

Our engineers install, commission and test the panel at your site — then hand over the User Manual, Maintenance Manual and warranty documentation. We remain available for post-installation support.

Testing Lab

In-House Testing Capability

Every panel is tested before it leaves our facility. All test equipment is periodically calibrated by NABL-approved third-party labs — as required under our ISO 9001:2015 certification.

HV Dielectric Test

High Voltage Dielectric Strength testing per IEC 61439 requirements

Megger Insulation Test

1000V and 500V Megger testing for insulation resistance across all circuits

Temperature Rise Test

In-house temperature rise test facility for current ratings up to 4000A

Torque & Conductivity

Nut torque tests at joints and conductivity tests on aluminium and copper busbars

Paint Thickness Test

Paint thickness and scratch test to verify powder coating quality and durability

Operational Tests

Secondary current injection tests and physical operational checks on all devices

PH & Solution Tests

PH and Titration point-age of solution per IS:3618 for busbar surface preparation

Calibrated Equipment

All test instruments periodically calibrated by third-party NABL labs — traceable to national standards

Quality Credentials

Our Certifications & Standards

ISO 9001:2015

Quality Management System certified for the design, manufacture and supply of electrical panels — ensuring consistent, world-class quality at every stage.

CPRI Certified

Central Power Research Institute type-tested certification for LV & MV switchboards — 5000A, 3-Phase, 4-Wire, 600V, 100kA short circuit withstand for 1 second.

IEC 61439

Full compliance with Part 1 (General Rules) and Part 2 (Power Switchgear and Control Gear Assemblies) — the leading international standard for panel boards.

IS:8623

Indian Standard compliance for factory-built assemblies of low-voltage switchgear and control gear — mandatory for all panels supplied to Indian projects.
